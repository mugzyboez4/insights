horsegiirL has built from near-zero to 318K weekly US streams in four years. The trajectory isn't linear — it moves in release-driven spikes that each establish a higher floor than the last.

Biggest jumps: MATERIAL HOR$E (Jan '25, +322K WoW), SEASALT & TEARDROPS (Aug '25, +171K), ONLY THE BEST (Feb '26, +177K). Each spike pulls the baseline up.

The US share at 35% is unusually high — most developing artists skew global. This is a US-first artist with secondary strength in Europe (Germany 145 idx, France 148, Poland 167, Denmark 263).

Catalog is doing heavy lifting between release spikes. My Little White Pony and My Barn My Rules (both 2022) are currently the 2nd and 3rd most-streamed tracks. Ensure catalog playlist positioning is maintained — it's what keeps the floor high between new singles.
